1. Introduction

StyleSync ("we," "our," or "us") is a social shopping application that helps users discover and share style with friends. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use StyleSync through the Shopify Shop app.

Developers: Revant Patel, Satvik Sandru, Samanyu Singh
Contact Email: revant.h.patel@gmail.com

By using StyleSync, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create a StyleSync profile, we collect:

  • Username - A unique identifier you choose (lowercase, alphanumeric)
  • Bio - Optional personal description you provide
  • Style Interests - Tags you select (e.g., Fashion, Streetwear, Vintage)
  • Gender Preference - Optional information you provide
  • Profile Visibility Settings - Whether your profile is public or private

2.2 Information from Shopify Shop App

Through the Shop Minis SDK, we automatically receive:

  • Display Name - Your name from your Shop app account
  • Profile Picture - Your avatar image from your Shop app account
  • Shop Public ID - A unique identifier from Shopify
  • Shop Follows - Information about shops you follow in the Shop app
  • Product Data - Product information from shops you follow (product images, titles, prices, URLs)

2.3 Information Generated Through Use

  • Friend Connections - Users you connect with as friends
  • Friend Requests - Sent and received friend requests
  • Card Profile Data - Your style card rank and suit based on your network
  • Usage Data - Basic app usage information necessary for functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Services: Create and maintain your StyleSync profile, enable friend connections and social features, display product feeds from shops you follow, calculate and display your style card (rank and suit)
  • Improve User Experience: Personalize your feed and recommendations, enable search and discovery features, facilitate friend discovery through public profiles
  • Maintain Security: Authenticate your identity, prevent fraud and abuse, ensure compliance with our Terms of Service

4. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database and hosting service. Supabase provides secure data storage with encryption, database management and backups, and Edge Functions for API processing.

Supabase's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their policies at https://supabase.com/privacy.

4.2 No Other Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties except:

  • Supabase (as described above)
  • As required by law or legal process

We do not sell your data, use it for targeted advertising, share it with analytics services, or use third-party tracking or analytics tools.
